Meet Jazzi B.

Hello! I'm Jazzi B. and I have a passion for traveling and seeing new places. I've been traveling ever since 8 years old with my local Boys & Girls Club. I've seen all of New England and other states in the USA. I've traveled to Dubai and India at the age of 16 years old for 2 weeks. I've cruised through Central America stopping in Mexico, Belize and Honduras. Puerto Rico, Canada, Jamaica and Spain have been my most recent trips traveled as an adult with my partner.

Traveling is important for numerous reasons. The first being that there is a lot more to see than your neighborhood and environment. The second being that there are so many places in the world to see. There are 7 continents, 196 countries that fall into 8 regions found around the world.
